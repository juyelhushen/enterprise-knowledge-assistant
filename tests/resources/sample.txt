Employee Handbook

Welcome to Acme Technologies.

Annual Leave
- Employees receive 20 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.
- Up to 5 unused leave days may be carried forward.

Work From Home
- Employees may work from home up to 2 days per week with manager approval.

Working Hours
- Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday.

Code of Conduct
- Treat colleagues with respect.
- Protect confidential company information.
